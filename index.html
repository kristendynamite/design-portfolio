Kristen McKee

Hi! I am a Front-end Developer and Web Designer based in the Portland, OR area. I was born in the Bay Area and raised in Texas. My alma mater is Santa Clara University. For several years, I worked in online advertising at Google. In 2013, I did some traveling before pursuing a new career in the beauty industry here in Portland. Working closely with local photographers and clothing designers as a makeup artist has woken up my creative side, and I love sharing my vision through web design.
