Hi! I am a UX/UI Designer based in Vancouver, WA. My alma mater is Santa Clara University, where I earned my BA in Communications. For several years, I lived in San Francisco and worked in Online Advertising at Google. In 2013, I left the Bay Area to pursue a new career in the beauty industry here in Portland. Working with local photographers and clothing designers as a Makeup Artist has woken up my creative side, and I love sharing my vision through web design.
